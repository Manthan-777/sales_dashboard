# Sales Performance Analysis Dashboard (Power BI)

## Project Overview
This project analyzes sales data to understand product performance, regional contribution, and sales trends over time.

## Tools Used
- Power BI
- Excel (data cleaning)

## Key Insights
- Identified top 10 products contributing highest sales
- Technology category shows the strongest growth trend
- West and East regions contribute the majority of total sales

## Dashboard Features
- Top 10 products by sales
- Sales trend by year and category
- Regional sales contribution
- Category-based filtering

## Dataset
Superstore-style sales dataset

## Note
Profit and profit margin were not analyzed due to absence of cost data.
